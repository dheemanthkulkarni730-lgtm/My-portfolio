🍽 Vegetarian Bulking Diet

Eat Smart • Train Hard • Grow Strong

🌅 Breakfast

  • 100g Oats with Milk
  • 2 Bananas
  • 2 tbsp Peanut Butter
  • 10 Almonds

🥛 Mid-Morning Snack

  • 1 Glass Milk
  • Handful of Mixed Nuts
  • 1 Apple

🍛 Lunch

  • 2 Cups Rice
  • Dal
  • 150–200g Paneer
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Curd

🍌 Pre-Workout

  • 1 Banana
  • Black Coffee (Optional)
  • Plenty of Water

💪 Post-Workout

  • 1 Scoop Whey Protein
  • 250ml Milk
  • 2 Bananas

🌙 Dinner

  • Chapati or Rice
  • 150–200g Paneer / Soya Chunks
  • Dal
  • Vegetables

😴 Before Bed

  • 1 Glass Milk
  • Handful of Nuts

💡 Bulking Tips

  • Eat 300–500 calories above maintenance.
  • Consume 1.6–2.2g protein per kg of body weight daily.
  • Drink at least 3 litres of water.
  • Sleep 8–9 hours every night.
  • Train consistently with progressive overload.
